How do I register for DevConf 2026?

Click "Register Now" at the top of the page and choose the Standard, Pro, or Team pass. Early-bird pricing ends August 15, 2026.

Are the sessions recorded?

Yes. Standard passes include 30 days of digital recordings, while Pro passes unlock lifetime access to every session.

Is there a group discount for teams?

Yes, the Team pass covers up to 10 people at $5,999, including a dedicated lounge and a recruitment booth for one day.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes. Every participant will receive a digital certificate of attendance after the conference ends.

Are meals included with my ticket?

Yes. All attendees will receive complimentary lunch, coffee breaks, and refreshments throughout the event.

What's the refund policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, passes can be transferred to a colleague at no extra cost.
